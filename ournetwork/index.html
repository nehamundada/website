About our network

It's hard to find good software development talent.

In the UK, most great software developers want control of their own destiny, which tends to lead them into contracting. We have embraced this trend and built a network from some of the best programmers, QAs, scrum masters and business analysts available. The people in our network each bring many years of experience of software development.

We have a diverse and inspirational collection of people who speak at conferences, write books, run open source projects, and have deep experience in many business domains and technologies.
